EVE-Taboo

Welcome to our little game of EVE-Taboo. Taboo is a word guessing game.
The objective is for a player to have the other players guess the word, which they're trying to describe.
The words you'll have to describe in our variant mainly revolve around EVE Online.
If you create a new game session you'll automatically be the game master and be the one who gets to decide who will be next.
